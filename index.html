Our Street Style Heritage

At Sree Samosas, we blend raw passion with high-speed spice mastery. Just like our signature recipe, we stand strong, bold, and deliver maximum rich satisfaction. Experience premium food preparation crafted with the finest local ingredients!

🌱 Core Ingredients

  • Outer Shell: All-purpose flour (Maida), carom seeds (Ajwain), clarified butter (Ghee), and warm water.
  • Classic Stuffing: Boiled mashed potatoes, green peas, green chilies, coriander leaves, and ginger.
  • Aromatic Spice Mix: Garam masala, cumin seeds, coriander powder, amchur (dry mango powder), and fennel.

📊 Nutrition Summary (Per Serving)

  • Energy: ~250 - 300 Calories
  • Macronutrients: 32g Carbohydrates, 4g Dietary Fiber, 5g Protein.
  • Healthy Elements: Iron from green peas, Vitamin C from green chilies, and energy-dense starches from potatoes.

✨ Unexpected Health Benefits

  • Metabolism Booster: Carom seeds (Ajwain) are famous for clearing acidity and easing stomach digestion.
  • Instant Energy Pack: Perfect carbohydrate reservoir to restore active calorie limits on exhaustive long days.
  • Immunity Spices: Cumin and coriander powders bring active antioxidant groups directly to the body.
